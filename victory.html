HAWKINS IS SAVED!

You find Eleven at the old Byers house. She looks tired but determined. "I can do this," she says. "But I'll need all of you with me."

Together, you return to the woods. The portal has grown larger, crackling with dangerous energy. Creatures stir on the other side.

Eleven steps forward, blood dripping from her nose. She raises her hand. "Friends... don't let go."

You all join hands, forming a circle around her. Mike holds her other hand. The power builds. The air vibrates.

"CLOSE!" Eleven screams.

With a blinding flash of light and a deafening crack, the portal implodes. The red glow vanishes. The creatures' screams fade into silence.

Eleven collapses, but she's smiling. "It's... done."

The sun begins to rise over Hawkins. The town is safe. Your friendship saved the world once again.
