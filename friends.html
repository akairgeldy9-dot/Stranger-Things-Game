The Party Assembles

You bike home as fast as you can. Once inside, you grab your walkie-talkie.

"Guys, this is a code red. I repeat, CODE RED. Over."

Within 20 minutes, Mike, Dustin, and Lucas arrive at your house with flashlights, backpacks, and determination. You tell them what you saw.

"Sounds like the Upside Down is back," Mike says grimly. "We need to investigate, but we need a plan."

Dustin pulls out a map. "We can check the woods together, or we could try to get into Hawkins Lab. They might have information about what's happening."
